NotedBot Commands

Normale Commands

!join
So that Joint The Bot in your channel • Please make sure that it is mod with /mod notedbot.
part
This will cause the bot to leave your channel.
NEW - setlanguage
Change the language of the bot. | The cmd is beta
ping
Shows you the ping and latency of the bot.
test
The bot sends a message to check if it is in the channel.
prefix
Change the prefix of the bot (!prefix "newPrefix"). If you forgot what the bot's prefix was, write "notedbot prefix?"
blockcmd
Deactivate/activate commands from the bot (!blockcmd disable test).
rechner
Calculate your math problems.
gpt
Write with a Ki.
globalannounce
Write !globalannounce badge/emote and the bot will send the new Twtich/7tv global badge/emotes/paints to your chat.
